# **App Name**: Rio Bonito Relief

## Core Features:

- Emergency Banner: Display a prominent, flashing banner indicating the emergency and its severity, including key statistics like casualties and displacement numbers.
- PIX Donation Panel: Provide a fixed, floating panel with the PIX key and QR code for immediate donations. Include suggested donation amounts and clear instructions.
- Transparency Dashboard: Show real-time fundraising progress, number of donors, and allocation of funds (e.g., shelter, food, medical supplies) via an infographic.
- News Feed Integration: Aggregate real-time updates from official sources like the Civil Defense and local news outlets to keep donors informed.
- Impact Visualizer: Show clear examples of what specific donation amounts can provide, such as food baskets or medical kits, to motivate donors.
- Credibility Showcase: Display trust elements like seals of approval from recognized organizations and video testimonials from victims.
- FAQ Section: Address common questions about donating via PIX, including confirmation, receipts, tax deductibility, and fund usage.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, calming blue (#3498DB), associated with trust and stability, to inspire confidence in donors.
- Background color: Soft, desaturated blue (#EBF5FB), creating a clean and unobtrusive backdrop for content.
- Accent color: Warm orange (#E67E22) to highlight calls to action and key information, conveying a sense of urgency and hope.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif that combines a modern look and a little warmth or personality; suitable for headlines or body text.
- Use clear, globally-recognized icons from a set like FontAwesome to represent different types of aid and support.  Follow standards set by organizations like the Red Cross for clear and immediate understanding.
- Mobile-first, prioritizing a clean and direct layout, with all critical information visible and accessible on smaller screens.
- Subtle animations on the progress bar and donation counters to provide dynamic feedback and engagement without distracting from the core message.
